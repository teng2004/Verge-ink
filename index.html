Pro-Tip for Fresh Ink

Avoid heavy sweating or touching the area with other skin for the first 6 hours. If you sleep within the first 12 hours, we recommend wearing long sleeves to prevent "transferring" the tattoo to your face or other parts!

Safety & FAQ

Since our ink dyes the top layer of your skin, it cannot be rubbed off instantly with alcohol or soap. It will naturally fade as your skin regenerates. To speed up the process, gently exfoliate with warm water and a body scrub or use a washcloth.
Our ink is made from Gardenia Jasminoides (plant-based). While it is organic and safe for most, we recommend a small patch test if you have extremely sensitive skin or plant allergies. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
Only during the first 24 hours of development! The ink is "active" until it fully darkens. To prevent "ghosting" (transferring the image to your face or sheets), we highly recommend wearing long-sleeved clothing during your first night of sleep.
